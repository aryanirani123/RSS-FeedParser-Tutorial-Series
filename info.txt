RSS FeedParser Tutorial Series 

What is RSS ?

RSS (Rich Site Summary) is a format for delivering regularly changing web content. 
News websites, weblogs, online publishers and more chain their content as an RSS 
Feed to whoever wants it.

What is FeedParser ?

Feedparser is a python module for downloading and parsing syndicated feeds.
In this video we are going to be using python and the feedparser module to 
get latest updates from your favourite medium bloggers.

What does this series contain ?

(1) Installing and getting started with FeedParser
(2) Interacting with the website(understanding the structure of the website, getting titles and more)
(3) FeedParser for Medium
(4) Advanced FeedParser for medium
